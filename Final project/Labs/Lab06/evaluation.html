Website Evaluation – Chelsea FC

URL of the Website

https://www.chelseafc.com/

Name of the Website

Chelsea Football Club Official Website

Target Audience

The site is made for people who support Chelsea FC.

Site Organization

The site has a simple layout with menus like Men's team, Women's team, Latest, Ticket & Hospitality, etc.

CRAP Design Principle Example

Repetition is used through the consistent blue & white design and layout.

Accessibility Audit Score

WAVE score is around 32%. Some buttons and links are not labeled correctly for screen readers.

Site Effectiveness

The site gives access to player details, match results, and news.

Site Efficiency

The site loads well on phones and computers.

Engagement

Images, videos, and stories make the site interesting.

Recommendations

The homepage could be improved by reducing large images and videos.
