Website Evaluation: Chelsea FC

URL of the Website

https://www.chelsefc.com

Name of the Website

Chelsea Football Club Official Website

Target Audience

The site is made for those who supports chelseafc, and also a big fan of chelsea football club.

Site Organization

Basically, this site has a simple layout with a menu that comprises of Men's team, Women's team, Latest, Ticket & Hospitality etc. It shows recent tories on the home page.

CRAP Design Principle Example

Repetition is used in the color theme and layout. Every page uses the same blue and white design and text style.

Accessibility Audit Score

WAVE Accessibility Checker score: around 32%. The site has around 47 accessibility issues. Some of the issues include interactive buttons and links that are not properly labeled for screen readers, and some headings that are not in the right order. These issues can make it harder for blind or low-vision users to navigate the site

Site Effectiveness

The website gives access to player details, match results, and club news. It helps users find what they need easily.

Site Efficiency

It loads very well on phones and computers. The top menu makes it simple to move between pages.

Engagement

Images, videos, and stories make the site interesting and fun for the chelseafc fans. it allows fans to wnat to scroll more.

Recommendation

I think the website could be improved by reducing the number of large images and videos on the homepage.it sometimes feels a little too busy, which can make it hard to focus on one thing. If they made the layout simpler and added clearer text labels, it would be easier for everyone to use, including people with disabilities.
