Website Evaluation: Chelsea FC

URL of the Website

https://www.chelsefc.com

Name of the Website

Chelsea Football Club Official Website

Target Audience

The site is made for those who supports chelseafc, and also a big fan of chelsea football club.

Site Organization

Basically, this site has a simple layout that comprises of Teams, Matches, Men team, Women team, News, Videos etc. It shows recent tories on the home page.

CRAP Design Principle Example

Repetition is used in the color theme and layout. Every page uses the same blue and white design and text style.

Accessibility Audit Score

WAVE Accessibility Checker score: around 92%. The site is mostly accessible but has minor color contrast issues.

Site Effectiveness

The website gives quick access to player details, match results, and club news. It helps users find what they need easily.

Site Efficiency

It loads fast and works well on phones and computers. The top menu makes it simple to move between pages.

Engagement

Images, videos, and stories make the site interesting and fun for fans. It keeps people wanting to scroll more.

Recommendation

The homepage could be a little cleaner by showing fewer videos or ads at once to make it easier to read.
