How Is PNG Used?

What Is It? Why Is It Important? How Is It Used? Sources

PNG is used for logos because it keeps edges sharp and allows for transparent backgrounds. This makes it easy to place a logo on any design or surface without a white box appearing behind it.

PNG is also used for screenshots, icons, and online graphics. pictures saved as PNG stay clear and are easy to read, which is helpful for tutorials or instructions.

You can also use PNG for your wallpapers and digital art because the colors stay strong and the details remain sharp. as you can see from the example i showed you, this pictures can be used for wallpaper on phones and laptps.
