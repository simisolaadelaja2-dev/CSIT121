What Is PNG? (Second Edition)

What Is It? Why Is It Important? How Is It Used? Sources

PNG (Portable Network Graphics) is an image format that keeps picture quality clear and sharp. It dosen't lose quality when it is saved or downloaded, which makes it different from formats like JPEG.

One of the most important features of PNG is that it supports transparent backgrounds. This means a PNG image can be placed on any design or webpage without showing a colored box around it. This makes it perfect for logos, icons, and graphics.

Another advantage of PNG is its ability to display small details clearly. Whether it contains text, shapes, or fine lines, a PNG file keeps everything crisp. PNG is also easy to download and transfer between two devices without losing quality.
