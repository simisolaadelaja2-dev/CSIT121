Why Is PNG Important?

What Is It? Why Is It Important? How Is It Used? Sources

PNG is important because it keeps images looking clear and high-quality. Many websites and designers use PNG when they need clear graphics that does not look blurry or bad. This helps gives pages clean and professional look.

The support for transparency also makes PNG useful. You can place a PNG image on any background without getting a colored box behind it. This flexibility makes PNG better than formats that do not support transparency.

Another reason PNG is important is that it works on all devices and browsers. Whether someone is using a phone, tablet, or computer, PNG images display the same way. This consistency makes the format reliable for web and digital use.
